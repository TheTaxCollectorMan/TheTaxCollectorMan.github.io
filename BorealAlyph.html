    • Boreal Alyph

    Boreal Alyph is an indie FPS title from Keep Away From Fire in the Half-Life universe. Licensed by Valve, my objectives on the project involved community management, social media management, the preparation of writing and video content for PR purposes, and third-party communication with Valve to negotiate access to our licensed code.

    You can learn more about Boreal Alyph and Keep Away From Fire on our website!

    VaultMaps

    VaultMaps is an anthology of month-long mapping competitions for the Source 2 engine, centralizing primarily around creating VR experiences for Half-Life: Alyx. As a co-founder and organizer, my objectives on the project involved hosting brainstorming sessions, working with third-party graphic designers to create brand and PR content, organizing and launching new competitions, and providing support to entrants.

    You can learn more about VaultMaps at its Twitter account!
